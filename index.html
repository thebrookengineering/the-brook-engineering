Trusted Engineering & Surveying Professionals

The Brook Engineering and Survey Services is a professional geomatics and engineering surveying consultancy based in Lusaka, Zambia. We support individuals, construction firms, institutions, and development organizations with accurate measurements, compliant survey outputs, and practical technical solutions.

Our work is grounded in professional standards, regulatory compliance, and a clear understanding of engineering and construction workflows within the Zambian context.

Professional Profile

The practice is led by a qualified Geomatics Engineer and Surveyor with seven (7) years of professional experience in engineering surveying, cadastral surveys, and construction support services.

Professional registrations and affiliations include the Engineering Institution of Zambia (EIZ), the Surveying Institute of Zambia (SIZ), and Chartered Managers Canada (CIM). These affiliations reflect a commitment to technical competence, ethical practice, and continuous professional development.

Our Core Services

Construction Setting Out

Accurate positioning and alignment of structures to ensure construction works follow approved designs.

Topographic Surveys

Detailed mapping of natural and man-made features to support planning and design.

Engineering Surveys

Specialized surveys for infrastructure projects such as roads, drainage, and structural works.

Cadastral Surveys

Boundary determination and land parcel surveys conducted in accordance with statutory requirements.

Site Plan Designs

Preparation of site plans for development approval, planning, and construction.

Quantity Surveying Support

Measurement and quantification support for construction cost estimation and control.
